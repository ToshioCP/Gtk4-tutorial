Once upon a time, there was an old man called Taketori-no-Okina.
His name is Japanese and means a man who makes bamboo baskets.
One day, he went into the hills and found a shining bamboo stalk.
“What a mysterious bamboo this is!” he said.
When he cut it open, he found a small, cute baby girl inside.
The girl was faintly glowing.
He thought the baby was a gift from heaven and took her home.
His wife was surprised by his story. They were very happy because they had no children.
